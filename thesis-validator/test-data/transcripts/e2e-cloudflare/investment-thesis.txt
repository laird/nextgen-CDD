Investment Thesis for Cloudflare
========================================

## Cloudflare Investment Thesis

**1. Dominant Market Position in Critical Infrastructure**
- Leading global edge computing and web security platform with 20%+ market share in CDN/security
- Mission-critical services create high switching costs and sticky customer relationships
- Network effects strengthen with scale as broader coverage improves service quality

**2. Secular Growth Tailwinds**
- Rising cybersecurity threats drive increasing demand for edge security solutions
- Cloud migration and digital transformation accelerating enterprise adoption
- Expanding total addressable market from ~$100B today to $200B+ by 2026

**3. Strong Competitive Moats**
- Massive global network infrastructure (275+ cities) extremely difficult to replicate
- Superior performance and cost efficiency vs. legacy providers and hyperscalers
- Developer-first approach and ease of integration create strong customer loyalty

**4. Scalable Business Model with Expanding Margins**
- High incremental margins as traffic scales across existing infrastructure
- Land-and-expand strategy driving 120%+ net revenue retention
- Growing mix of higher-margin Zero Trust and developer platform services

**5. Key Risk: Intense Competition**
- Well-funded competitors (AWS, Microsoft, Google) with deep pockets
- Potential margin pressure from pricing competition
- Execution risk in rapidly evolving cybersecurity landscape
